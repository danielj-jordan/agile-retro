Privacy Policy

What information does Algileretro.io collect?

Agileretro.io collects common web log information.

The purpose of AgileRetro.io is to facilitate sprint retrospectives. As part of this process, comments are entered into the system and shared with others on their team. These comments are collected from the users as are the names of sprints, category comments and related information.

If you sign in with Google Id, then your Google Id, and Name are also collected.

How does Agileretro.io use this information?

AgileRetro.io uses the weblog information to monitor website performance. The information that you supply to AgileRetro.io through the application is used to help facilitate sprint retrospectives for your team.

What information does Agilereto.io share?

If you sign in with Google Id, then AgileRetro.io shares that information with Google. No other information is shared with third parties.
