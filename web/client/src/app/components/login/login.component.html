AgileRetro.io

AgileRetro.io is a tool to facilitate sprint retrospectives for agile software development teams.

The team members contribute comments in different categories such as:

  • What went well?
  • What can be improved
  • What action items does the team commit to for the following sprint?

    • The category names in Agileretro.io are defined by the leader of the team who is also known as the scrum master. The team members submit their comments for each category and then discuss and commit to the action items during a meeting known as the sprint retrospective. Agileretro.io is a tool to gather those comments before and during the sprint retrospective.
